As a lifelong learner, I've adopted a growth mindset. As a teaching professional, I had to identify and address daily challenges, learning and adapting with each possible solution. Each year, I dedicated time to self-directed learning, utilizing various resources to enhance both my teaching methodologies and my students' overall learning experiences. I believe in leading by example, demonstrating that it is never too late to pursue one's interests.

I enjoy various hobbies. I've expanded my baking skills through classes at the King Arthur Baking School in Vermont.
